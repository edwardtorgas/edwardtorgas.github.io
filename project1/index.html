Arithmetic

This page will give an overview of addition and multiplication.

Addition

Addition is the hardest easiest part of math to learn.

factor1 + factor2 = sum of the two factors

Examples:

  • 3 + 4 = 7
  • 23 + 32 =55
  • 44 + 52 = 96
